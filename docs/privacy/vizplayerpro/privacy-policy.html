Privacy Policy

App Name: Viz Player Pro

Developer: Vizworkz

Effective Date: 24-09-2025

1. Introduction

Viz Player Pro respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data when you use our video player and editor application.

2. Data Access

Our app requires access to video files and folders on your device to provide full functionality, including:

  • Playing videos selected by you
  • Editing videos in directories you choose
  • Saving edited videos to locations specified by you

The app does not access system files or any content outside of the directories you select.

3. Data Collection and Sharing

We do not collect, store, or share any personal data from your device. All operations occur locally on your device. Your files remain private and secure.

4. User Control

You have full control over which files and folders the app can access. You can revoke access at any time by changing folder permissions or uninstalling the app.

5. Offline Functionality

Viz Player Pro works fully offline. No internet connection is required for playback or editing, ensuring your videos remain private and secure on your device.

6. Security

  • All app executables are signed and distributed securely via the Microsoft Store.
  • All video processing occurs locally on your device; no data is transmitted externally without your consent.
  • The app only operates on files explicitly selected by the user.

7. Updates

Privacy policy changes will be updated in the app and on this page. Users are encouraged to review this policy periodically.
